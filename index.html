About Us

At Coyote Bikes, we are more than just a brand; we are a community of passionate riders who live and breathe the spirit of adventure. Founded by avid mountain biking enthusiasts, we embarked on this journey with a vision to share the exhilaration of exploring the great outdoors, just as the wild coyote roams its territory.
Our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability drives us to create exceptional mountain bikes that are not only high-performance but also environmentally responsible. We're dedicated to reducing our footprint on the planet, preserving the trails we love.
As a brand, we are your partner in adventure, offering more than just bikes. We're here to inspire, educate, and support you on your journey. Through our online resources, community events, and customization options, we aim to foster connections and help you embrace the wild spirit of mountain biking.
Coyote Bikes is not just a manufacturer; we're a movement, and we invite you to be a part of it. Join us on a journey where the trail is endless, and the possibilities are boundless. Experience the wilderness. Embrace the adventure. Choose Coyote Bikes.
